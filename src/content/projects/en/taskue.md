## What it is

A minimalist to-do list designed for the keyboard. No accounts, no projects, no noise. Type something, hit Enter, done.

## Philosophy

Most productivity apps fail because they add friction exactly when you want to capture something. Taskue is built around **instant capture**: type and press Enter.

## Shortcuts

- **Enter** — add task
- **Escape** — clear field
- **Ctrl+T** — focus input
- **/** — search tasks

## Privacy

Your tasks live in your browser, locally. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your data never leaves your device.

## Install

Works as a PWA — installable from Chrome, Edge, Brave, Safari, mobile Firefox. Google Play and App Store coming soon.
