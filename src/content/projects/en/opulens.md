## What it is

OpuLens delivers enterprise-ready multimodal AI. One platform unifies visual, audio, and text analysis that previously required three separate solutions, with low latency and enterprise-grade privacy.

## Capabilities

- **Visual intelligence**: object detection and scene understanding in images and video.
- **Audio & text**: social media monitoring and stream processing.
- **Multimodal reasoning**: combines the three channels simultaneously, with real-time alerts.
- **Infrastructure**: 17 edge nodes across 6 continents, AES-256, TLS 1.3, zero-trust architecture (SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR).

## For whom

Operations, analytics, and engineering teams that need to process visual, audio, and text sources without building an AI stack from scratch.
